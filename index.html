Adobe Experience Manager

Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) is a web content management system that allows companies to offer individualized digital experience to customers across different delivery channels. Key Components of AEM include Digital Asset Management (DAM), Web Content Management (WCM), Multi Site Manager (MSM), and Adobe Experience Manager Apps.

Using Adobe Experience Manager, authors can create, edit and manage web pages/sites in many languages for different brands and multiple regions.

Integrating AEM with Adobe Marketing Cloud (or third party marketing tools) will enhance user experience and reach target audience better.
